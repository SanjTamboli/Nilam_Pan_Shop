Our Story

Nilam Pan Shop was founded in 2020 with a passion for delivering the freshest pan (betel leaf) and authentic masala varieties to our community. Starting as a small family venture, we've grown by focusing on quality ingredients, traditional recipes, and customer satisfaction. Our journey began in a local market, and today, we're proud to serve fresh, hygienic products that bring joy to every bite.

Our Mission

To provide the finest, hand-picked pan and masala products while promoting healthy, traditional habits. We aim to be the go-to shop for quality and freshness, ensuring every customer leaves with a smile and a great experience.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fresh Ingredients: Sourced daily from trusted farms for unbeatable quality.
  • Authentic Flavors: Traditional recipes with no artificial additives.
  • Customer-Centric: Personalized service and easy online ordering.
  • Affordable Prices: Value for money without compromising on taste.
  • Hygienic Packaging: Safe and eco-friendly options for all products.

About the Founder

Gous Tamboli is the visionary behind Nilam Pan Shop. With over 15 years of experience in the food industry, Gous has a deep love for traditional Indian flavors. His dedication to sourcing the best betel leaves and spices has made Nilam a trusted name. When not at the shop, Gous enjoys community events and sharing recipes with locals.

About the Co-Founder

Irfan Tamboli co-founded Nilam Pan Shop alongside Gous, bringing her expertise in marketing and customer relations. A passionate advocate for healthy eating, Irfan ensures our products meet the highest standards. Her innovative ideas have helped expand our online presence, making it easier for customers to order. Irfan is also involved in local charities and loves experimenting with new masala blends.
